Final Project

This interactive photo gallery highlights a spectrum of vibrant colors that play a major role in visual storytelling. Each photo represents a unique tone and feeling, from bold reds to calming blues, curated to inspire creativity and appreciation of color.

The gallery is fully responsive and built with accessibility in mind. It adapts seamlessly across devices—from mobile phones to large screens—and responds to user preferences such as reduced motion and dark mode. Whether browsing casually or exploring specific hues, users can enjoy a smooth and inclusive experience.
